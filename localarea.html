Visit Our Local Area

As your holidaying with us at Oakwood Cove Camp site, we encourage you to visit the local area and really get a feel for what our corner of North Wales has to offer. If you'd like any recommendations, please speak to our friendly reception team or contact us. We've highlighted some gems below!

Glyndwr Town

Glyndwr is a small idyllic village located in North Wales, which sits under the county of Gwynedd and has a population of about 1,000 people. It is relatively easy to get to, with it being just 7 miles away from Bangor and not far from the A55. The village also has an active Lifeboat Station.
