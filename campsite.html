Our Pitches

We have a few different type of pitches to suit a variety of needs and all are suitable for a standard sized tent, caravan or motorhome.

  1. Grass only Pitch with no electric hook-up
  2. Grass Pitch with electric hook-up
  3. Hardstanding Pitch with electric hook-up. No fresh water supply or waste drainage
  4. Super Pitch; a Hardstanding Pitch with electric hook-up, as well as fresh water supply and waste drainage (additional fee of £4.50 payable per night)
  5. Ready to Camp Tents
  6. Luxury Glamping Pods (additional fee of £4.50 payable, per night)
