A beautiful retreat nestled along the serene coastline of Angelsey, North Wales

Welcome to our Camp Site

We offer a perfect fusion of tranquillity and adventure for nature enthusiasts, beach lovers and gentle wanderers alike, as we are situated between the picturesque Llŷn Peninsula Beach and the charming local town of Glyndwr. We offer a laid-back atmosphere where you can unwind and connect with nature while still having easy access to explore the vibrant surroundings. Discover quaint shops, cosy cafes, and friendly locals. We also have easy access to the local beach, which provides fantastic views of landscape.

Timings

Check In: 13:00
Check Out: 12:00

Our open season is from March to November. We are closed in the winter months.

Prices

Prices range from £20 to £35 per night.
